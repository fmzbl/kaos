The tunnel, read as law

Every symbol the adept holds in context is a sigil, and its charge — how many characters it keeps when the transcript is re-rendered each turn — is its depth in the tunnel. One Fibonacci ladder descends from the statement of intent at the deep end: the first sigil, the most charged, never compressed at all. Its reverse twin ascends to the freshest observation at the tunnel mouth. Ring by ring the charge falls 8 · 5 · 3 · 2 · 1 — each rung φ dimmer than the last, which is what makes the decay Fibonacci — until only base charge remains in the long dim middle. A symbol takes the brighter of its two universes. Implemented in src/charge.rs.

Polarity — the second axis

When a sigil must be cut to its budget, its sign decides which end survives. The cut itself has a reverse twin.

+ positive · keeps its HEAD

$ ls sympy/core/ basic.py kind.py mul.py numbers.py power.py symbol.py def kind(self): """The kind of this object.""" …(1,846 chars banished)

A clean read, a passing run — openings orient. The head names the files, the shape, the signature.

− negative · keeps its TAIL

…(3,178 chars banished) File "tests.py", line 41, in check assert net == Decimal("20.00") AssertionError: Decimal('100.00') != Decimal('20.00') exit 1

Tracebacks put the punchline last. An error's tail is the one line the next action depends on.

Banishment

A reply with no <act> block never enters the tunnel at all — only a one-line nudge remains, so a format stumble cannot rot the context it came from. Three consecutive banishments end the session. The repetition ward works the same way: an identical re-read of an unchanged file collapses to one line ("act on what you already know"), and any edit to the file lifts the ward.

The measured motive

Before the ladders, the transcript grew append-only until it drowned: benchmark traces showed the model re-reading the same file eight consecutive turns, and one instance lost to step-exhaustion with zero edits made. The intent was drifting out of a context stuffed with stale reads. The tunnel keeps the two mouths burning — why the adept was summoned and what just happened — and lets everything between decay by φ per ring.
